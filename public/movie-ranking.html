The Best Reto Cartoons According to Selorm

My top 3 Retro Cartoons of all-time.

Mona the Vampire

I love her adventures and how weird and quriky she was. The art style was fun too.

Angela Anaconda

This had quite amazing cut style animation. Angela and her adventures were also very funny.

Monster by Mistake

Monster by Mistake had such a fresh style and idea about the show that made it so addicting!
